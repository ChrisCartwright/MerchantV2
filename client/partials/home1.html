As a Rentee

Need a pair of skates for the afteernoon but don't want to pay inflated prices from a large department store? Check Merchant for nearby items available for rent. Lower prices and more convenient locations make Merchant the best choice.

Security

In order to keep your items safe, specific locations will not be released until both the renter and the rentee have contacted each other and a deposit has been placed. The deposit is kept by Merchant until both parties have agreed that the item has been returned. This will ensure that your item is kept safe and the item is actually what it was advertised as.
